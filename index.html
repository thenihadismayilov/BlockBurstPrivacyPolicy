PRIVACY POLICY

Last Updated: March 31, 2026

1. Introduction

Block Burst ("we," "our," or "us"), developed and operated by an individual developer, is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what limited information we collect, how we use it, and how we protect it when you use our mobile application.

By using Block Burst, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information We Collect

Block Burst is designed with your privacy in mind. We collect only the minimum data necessary to operate the game's leaderboard feature. Specifically, we collect:

  • - Game Score: Your in-game score at the time it is submitted to the leaderboard.
  • - Device Identifier (UIDevice ID): A unique identifier generated by your iOS device, used solely to distinguish your entry on the leaderboard and display "You" next to your score.

We do not collect your name, email address, location, contact list, photos, microphone data, or any other personal information.

3. How We Use Your Information

The data we collect is used exclusively for the following purpose:

  • - Leaderboard Display: Your score and device identifier are sent to Firebase to maintain a global ranking list. The device identifier allows the app to identify and highlight your own entry on the leaderboard with the label "You."

We do not use your data for advertising, marketing, profiling, or any purpose beyond the leaderboard functionality described above.

4. Data Storage and Third-Party Services

Your game score and device identifier are stored using Google Firebase, a cloud platform provided by Google LLC. Firebase is used to securely store and retrieve leaderboard data in real time. Data stored in Firebase is subject to Google's own privacy and security standards.

For more information on how Google handles data, please refer to Google's Privacy Policy at https://policies.google.com/privacy .

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your data to any third parties. The only data sharing that occurs is the transmission of your game score and device identifier to Firebase for leaderboard purposes, as described in Section 3. We may disclose data only in the following limited circumstances:

  • - Legal Requirements: When required by applicable law, court order, or governmental request.
  • - Business Transfers: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or transfer of assets, where users will be notified of any relevant changes.

6. Data Retention

Leaderboard entries (score and device identifier) are retained on Firebase for as long as necessary to maintain the in-game rankings. Since no account is created, there is no account deletion flow; however, you may contact us at the email below to request removal of your data from the leaderboard.

7. Notifications

Block Burst may request permission to send you push notifications. You can enable or disable notifications at any time from the Settings screen within the app, or through your device's system notification settings. Disabling notifications will not affect your ability to play the game or access the leaderboard. No personal data is collected through the notifications feature.

8. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect the limited data we collect. Leaderboard data is transmitted and stored using Firebase's industry-standard security infrastructure. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. Children's Privacy

Block Burst is a casual puzzle game suitable for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. The only data we collect is an anonymous device identifier and game score, neither of which constitutes personal information under applicable children's privacy laws such as COPPA. Parents or guardians with questions are welcome to contact us at the address below.

10. International Data Transfers

Your leaderboard data (score and device identifier) may be stored on Firebase servers located outside your country of residence. By using Block Burst, you consent to this transfer. We rely on Firebase's security infrastructure to ensure your data is handled appropriately regardless of where it is stored.

11. Your Rights

Depending on your country of residence, you may have the right to:

  • - Access the data we hold about you
  • - Request deletion of your leaderboard data
  • - Object to processing of your data

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at: support@blockburst.com

12. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any significant changes will be communicated via an update to this page or through an in-app notice. The "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy reflects when changes were most recently made. Continued use of Block Burst after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
